Projects

Steel Crate Games

Steel Crate Games is an independent studio located in Ottawa, Canada. Ben is one of three co-founders and co-created the studio's debut title Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes. The game focuses on fostering interesting communication between players and won Excellence in Design at the Independent Games Festival (2016).

Steel Crate Games is currently Ben's full-time focus.

Going Loud Studios

Going Loud Studios is Ben's solo independent game development company. Its most well-known title, DLC Quest, won Xbox LIVE Indie Game of the Year (2011) from Official Xbox Magazine and is available on Steam.

Going Loud Studios is mostly quiet, but did recently release Loot Box Quest on Steam.

Indie Chatter

Camera, coffee, and indie games. Indie Chatter is a YouTube channel about independent game development with short videos published over the course of about a year and half. Ben covered everything from programming to marketing to musing about the industry and even answered questions pulled from a hat.

Indie Chatter came to a close in 2014.

Publications

Procedural Generation in Game Design
